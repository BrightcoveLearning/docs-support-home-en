Introduction

The Brightcove AI Content Multiplier can help you repurpose your long-form content to reach your audience with shorter videos.

Creating new clips

  1. Navigate to the media module and select your video.

    Source videos should be at least 3 minutes long.
  2. Click on the AI Button and select Clips.

    Content you can generate with this process:

    • Key Moments: Your video's most relevant moments.
    • Summaries: Key moments stitched together.
    • Reels: Key moments in vertical format.
    • Chapters: Longer video sections based on topic.

    Key moments are selected based on relevant topics, audio intensity, and visual queues to predict your most engaging snippets.
  3. Fill in your video's information, along with the output to generate, and click Start

  4. Once the process is completed, you will be able to see your new content as separate entities from your original video in your media module.

    Please allow this process some time to complete.

    When little or no metadata is provided, the new asset name will be generated with a content multiplier format.
    This can be edited after the requested videos are created.

    5. Cropping a video to a vertical format

    1. Click the AI Button and select Crop to vertical.

    2. Select your Video Type and click Start

    3. Once the process is completed, you will be able to see your new content as separate entities from your original video in your media module.

      Please allow this process some time to complete.
