Introduction

To make your content more discoverable, our new AI-Metadata Optimizer leaves no stone unturned. No more manual input, lots more engagement and optimization.

Using the AI-Metadata Optimizer

  1. Navigate to the media module
  2. Select your video
  3. If you do not have text tracks, Add text tracks to your video.
    If you do not have text tacks in english you cannot proceed with the next steps.
  4. In the overview tab, Click Generate with AI
  5. Select and edit your metadata
    • you can generate more responses
    • you can edit your metadata
  6. Click on the checkmark to select
    Only the selected metadata will be saved to your video.
  7. Click Save
